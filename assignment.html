What is web accessibility

Web accessibility refers to the practice of designing and developing websites, web applications, and other digital content that can be accessed and used by people with disabilities or different needs, without barriers or limitations.

Write a Short note on tabindex

The tabindex attribute is primarily intended to allow tabbable elements to have a custom tab order (specified in positive numerical order), instead of just being tabbed through in their default source order.
